Products

Rockbridge Farm offers a variety of sheep and wool products from our Black Welsh Mountain and Romney flocks.

Fleeces

We offer raw and washed fleeces from both our Black Welsh Mountain and Romney flocks. Black Welsh Mountain fleeces are completely black, dense, and durable — prized by hand spinners. Romney fleeces are heavy and thick, known for being easy to spin and ideal for a wide range of fiber arts projects.

Yarn

Our all natural yarns are produced from the wool of our own sheep. Each skein reflects the character of the breed it came from — the rich dark tones of Black Welsh Mountain wool or the versatile natural shades of Romney.

Blankets

Warm, durable blankets made from the natural wool of our flock. These blankets showcase the quality and character of wool raised right here on the farm.
